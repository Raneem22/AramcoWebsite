About Us

Community Services
Through its 7 departments, operates and maintains the Saudi Aramco residential communities and office buildings and provides comprehensive maintenance/life support to the non-industrial facilities and infrastructures of the Company. We're a dedicated service organization, and we're part of your everyday life.
We're a team of around 1,700 Saudi Aramco professionals, supported by over 14,000 contractor employees, who strive to provide you a desirable environment in which to work and live. We operate in the Eastern, Central and Western regions of Saudi Arabia. We're proud to be an integral part of the world's largest oil company - Saudi Aramco. Many of our services are very visible, such as recreation and dining facilities, beautifully landscaped garden areas and manicured sports fields. However, we also maintain the offices you work in and the homes you live in and provide you with water and AC services. We're behind the scenes, working 24 hours a day, to ensure that these services you depend on are always available.
Mission
Develop and manage high-quality community and office services… in a reliable, innovative and cost-effective manner… providing a desirable environment in which to work and live.
Vision
Create a safe, healthy and attractive environment for retaining a talented workforce by providing high quality services in our communities and offices in a reliable, innovative and cost-effective manner.
